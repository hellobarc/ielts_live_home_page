IELTS Mock Test

IELTS mock test is a test that is designed according to the actual IELTS exam for practice purpose. By taking part in the mock tests, you can hone your skill for getting used to with the actual exam.
An IELTS mock test includes all 4 modules exactly like the actual exam, giving you the chance to practice at home or in an IELTS preparation center. The online mock tests are computer based and also included the checking of the papers from the native English teachers.
In fact, mock test is crucial in taking IELTS preparation as there is no other way of judging your level of English regarding the IELTS exam. In order to take part in an IELTS mock test online, a you have to register on IELTS.live. You will be given the questions online where you have to give the answers there using the online tool provided by our website.

About

IELTS Live is a web-based application built by British American Resource Centre (BARC) as part of our integrated online learning environment.
It has been made accessible not only to our students but also to IELTS candidates all over the world. IELTS Live is the system where candidates can take IELTS mock tests, see their band scores, learn from their mistakes and get useful tips from some of our highly experienced instructors at BARC. Extra measures have been taken towards ensuring the application’s user-friendliness and quality of each feedback, in order to provide beneficial learning experiences for the IELTS candidates.
